Welcome to Surbhi Metal and Alloys - A perfect solution for all your metal requirements. Surbhi Metal and Alloys having it's registered office in Mumbai. Surbhi Metal and Alloys is a team of experienced industry experts who deal in manufacturing, supplying and trading of ferrous and non ferrous metals.

We manufacture and supply a variety of ferrous and non ferrous metals such as iron, stainless steel, aluminium, copper, brass, etc.

We manufacture and trade various products such as pipes, sheets, bars, flanges, fasteners, valves, fitting items and many other products applicable in various core sectors.

Our products have application in various industries such as Oil and Gas, Marine, Automobiles, Construction, Textiles, Telecom, Food Processing and many other industries. We serve all over India in various prominent companies and are proud to have a strong client base.

We believe in building trustful relationships with our clients by providing them with best quality material at a very reasonable rate.
Quality is our prime focus and we never compromise with it under any circumstance .We duly consider all the chemical compositions and strictly follow international standards for providing quality products. With an advanced set of machineries and a qualified team of experts we are able to cater to a varied demand in the industry.
