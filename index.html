About Us

Welcome to Surbhi Metal and Alloys - A perfect solution for all your metal requirements. Surbhi Metal and Alloys having it's registered office in Mumbai. Surbhi Metal and Alloys is a team of experienced industry experts who deal in manufacturing, supplying and trading of ferrous and non ferrous metals. We manufacture and supply a variety of ferrous and non ferrous metals such as iron, stainless steel, aluminium, copper, brass, etc. We manufacture and trade various products such as pipes, sheets, bars, flanges, fasteners, fitting items and many other products applicable in various core sectors.

Our Quality Policy
